An article is any member of a class of dedicated words that are used with noun phrases to mark the identifiability of the referents of the noun phrases. The category of articles constitutes a part of speech. It is quite a common activity for students to write something intended for publication. That task can mean writing an article, an entry for a competition, and a review, and all possible write-ups that can be published in an English magazine.
